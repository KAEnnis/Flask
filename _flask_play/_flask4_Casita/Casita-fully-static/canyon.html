The Canyon Room

The Canyon Room is a luxurious and spacious suite located on the top floor of Casita Sedona. The room features floor-to-ceiling windows with stunning views of the Sedona red rocks. The Canyon Room is perfect for couples or small groups looking for a romantic and relaxing getaway.

The room is tastefully decorated in a Southwestern style with warm colors and soft furnishings. The king-size bed is dressed in luxurious linens and the bathroom features a large soaking tub and separate shower. The Canyon Room also has a private balcony with outdoor seating, perfect for enjoying the views and the fresh air.

The Canyon Room comes with a variety of amenities to make your stay as enjoyable as possible, including:

  • A minibar
  • A coffee maker
  • A flat-screen TV with cable channels
  • A Blu-ray player
  • A sound system
  • Free Wi-Fi
  • A welcome basket
  • A turndown service

The Canyon Room is also located close to all of the amenities that Casita Sedona has to offer, including the spa, fitness center, swimming pool, and hiking trails. Whether you're looking to relax and unwind or explore the Sedona area, the Canyon Room is the perfect place to stay.
