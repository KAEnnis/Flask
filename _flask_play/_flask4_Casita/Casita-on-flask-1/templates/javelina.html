Javelina Room

The Javelina Room is a cozy and comfortable room located on the ground floor of Casita Sedona. The room features large windows with views of the Sedona desert. It's perfect for couples or small groups looking for a budget-friendly and relaxing getaway.

The room is tastefully decorated in a Southwestern style with warm colors and soft furnishings. The queen-size bed is dressed in luxurious linens and the bathroom features a large walk-in shower. The Javelina Room also has a private patio with outdoor seating, perfect for enjoying the views and the fresh air.

The Javelina Room comes with a variety of amenities to make your stay as enjoyable as possible, including:

  • A coffee maker
  • A flat-screen TV with cable channels
  • A DVD player
  • Free Wi-Fi
  • A welcome basket

The Javelina Room is also located close to all of the amenities that Casita Sedona has to offer, including the spa, fitness center, swimming pool, and hiking trails. Whether you're looking to relax and unwind or explore the Sedona area, the Javelina Room is the perfect place to stay.
