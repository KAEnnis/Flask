Festival Events

Here are a few upcoming festivals in Sedona between September and December 2023:

  • Sedona Jazz Festival - October 7-9, 2023: Enjoy three days of live jazz music from some of the best musicians in the world.
  • Sedona Rock Climbing Festival - November 4-6, 2023: Test your skills on some of the best rock climbing in the world.
  • Sedona Christmas Festival - December 1-3, 2023: Enjoy a festive weekend of Christmas shopping, caroling, and more.

For more information on these events and other upcoming festivals in Sedona, please visit the Sedona Chamber of Commerce website.
