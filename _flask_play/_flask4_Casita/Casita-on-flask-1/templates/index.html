About our Resort

Casita Sedona is a luxurious and secluded destination for those seeking peace, tranquility, and relaxation. Located in the heart of Sedona, Arizona, our resort offers stunning views of the red rocks and offers a variety of activities and amenities to help you rejuvenate and reconnect with yourself.

Our resort features:

  • Spacious and luxurious accommodations
  • A variety of dining options
  • A state-of-the-art spa
  • A fitness center
  • A swimming pool
  • A variety of hiking and biking trails
  • A meditation garden

Whether you're looking to escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life or simply reconnect with nature, Casita Sedona is the perfect place for you. Book your stay today and experience the Casita Sedona difference!
