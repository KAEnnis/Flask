Weekend Events

Looking for something to do on the weekend? Here are a few upcoming events in the vicinity of Casita Sedona:

  • Sedona Conference on Sustainable Tourism - October 13-15, 2023: Learn about the latest trends in sustainable tourism and how to make your travel more eco-friendly.
  • Sedona Film Festival - November 10-12, 2023: Enjoy three days of independent films from around the world.

For more information on these events and other upcoming events in Sedona, please visit the Sedona Chamber of Commerce website.
